Integral of the week

Let $$I_n:=\int \frac{dx}{x^{n}\sqrt{ax+b}}\, dx$$ with $n>1$ \begin{align*} I_n&= \int \frac{dx}{x^{n}\sqrt{ax+b}}=\int \frac{x}{x^{n+1}\sqrt{ax+b}}\,dx=\frac{1}{a}\int \frac{ax+b-b}{x^{n+1}\sqrt{ax+b}}\,dx \\ &=\int \left(\frac{1}{a}\frac{\sqrt{ax+b}}{x^{n+1}}-\frac{b}{a}\frac{1}{x^{n+1}\sqrt{ax+b}}\right) \,dx=\underbrace{\int \frac{1}{a}\frac{\sqrt{ax+b}}{x^{n+1}}\,dx}_{\text{Integration by parts}}-\frac{b}{a} I_{n+1}\\ &=\frac{-1}{a}\frac{\sqrt{ax+b}}{x^n n}+\frac{1}{2n}\int \frac{dx}{x^n \sqrt{ax+b}}-\frac{b}{a}I_{n+1} =\frac{-1}{a}\frac{\sqrt{ax+b}}{x^n n}+\frac{1}{2n}I_n-\frac{b}{a}I_{n+1} \\ &\implies I_{n+1}=\frac{-1}{b}\frac{\sqrt{ax+b}}{x^n n}+\frac{a}{b}I_n \left(\frac{1}{2n}-1\right)=\frac{-1}{b}\frac{\sqrt{ax+b}}{x^n n}-\frac{a}{b}I_n \left(\frac{2n-1}{2n}\right) \end{align*} Now, let us see the case $n$: $$I_n =\frac{-1}{b}\frac{\sqrt{ax+b}}{x^{n-1} (n-1)}-\frac{a}{b}I_{n-1} \left(\frac{2(n-1)-1}{2(n-1)}\right)=\frac{-\sqrt{ax+b}}{(n-1)bx^{n-1}}-\frac{a}{b}\frac{2n-3}{2n-2}\int \frac{dx}{x^{n-1}\sqrt{ax+b}}$$
